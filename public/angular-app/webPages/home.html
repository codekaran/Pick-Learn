About Pick&Learn


Pick&Learn is an online voting portal that has been designed to provide students the facility to caste a vote in order to select their subject teachers at the beginning of each semester.


Pick&Learn Description of each subject teacher is provided to help the student finalize his/her decision so as to promote institutional transparency and make the voting procedure easier.
